CA2

CA2 consists of 3 different assignments each about a part of digital fabrication. 3D Printing, Laser cutting and Programming.

Assignment 1

The first assignmemnt was to 3D print a phone stand that can hold your phone
I started by sketching the phone holder in 2D before using the OFFSET modifier to add thickness to my sketch.
I then extruded the sketch to slightly taller than the width of my phone.
I then moved over to Cura for prepare for 3D printing and printed it out.

Assignment 2

For assignment 2, the task was to make a accessory box.
I started by adding values and equations that would be important into my user parameters
I then used the values to create my sketches which are used for the laser cutting
After lasercutting using the sketches, I put the pieces together to make my final product

Assignment 3

For assignment 3, I was to code different programs to do different things with arduino.
